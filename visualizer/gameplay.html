Pathbreaker

Objective: Drain the robot's battery before it reaches the end.
Rules
  • Robot moves one step each turn and loses 1 battery per move.
  • Your objective is to drain the robot's battery before it reaches the end.
  • After each robot move, you may move exactly one obstacle.
  • You can move an obstacle only to one of its neighboring cells.
  • An obstacle move is illegal if it blocks all paths from Start to End.
  • An obstacle cannot be moved onto Start, End, the robot, or another obstacle.
  • An obstacle cannot immediately move back to where it came from in the previous turn.
  • Robot wins if it reaches End. You win if the robot cannot move.
